Amine Samir Boukhari (born 15 March 1998) is a French professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Ligue 1 club Olympique de Marseille and the France national team. Known for his technical ability, vision, and precise passing, Boukhari is considered one of the most promising midfield talents in French football.

Born in Lyon to Algerian parents, Boukhari began his youth career at local club FC Lyon before joining the prestigious Olympique Lyonnais academy at age 14. He progressed through Lyon's youth system and made his professional debut in 2018. After three successful seasons with Lyon, he transferred to Marseille in 2021 for a reported fee of €35 million, where he has established himself as a key player in midfield.

Boukhari made his senior international debut for France in 2019 and has since earned 45 caps, scoring 12 goals. He was part of the French squad that reached the semi-finals of UEFA Euro 2024.
