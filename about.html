I am currently under the supervision of Eric Verhoogen and Joseph Stiglitz.

I have a BA in Economics from UNAM and completed an exchange quarter at UC Davis. I was previously a predoc for David Escamilla-Guerrero at the University of St Andrews.

I'm particularly interested in research involving labor, inequality, firms, productivity, mechanism design, applied econometrics, and systems of production. I aim to pursue an academic career (that hopefully involves tenure) after my current appointments.
