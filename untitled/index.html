Reinufyodoria

Reinufyodoria is the world of an unnamed fantasy setting. Its surface is shaped by a simulated system of 44 tectonic plates whose collisions raise mountain belts and whose drift carries the 6 continents across the globe. Drag to spin the globe, scroll to zoom, toggle the flat map, and click a continent to open its article.
