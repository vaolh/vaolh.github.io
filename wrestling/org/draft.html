The Draft

The Draft is a rotating system.

The Draft is an annual event in which two of the sanctioning bodies take turns selecting from a pool of elegible wrestlers from the three bodies, while the third is allowed to sign any available free agents. The three sanctioning bodies, the WWF, the WWO, and IWB, each participate in a rotating in a rotating three-year cycle.
