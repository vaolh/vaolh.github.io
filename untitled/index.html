Touskeil

Touskeil is the world of an unnamed fantasy setting. Its surface is shaped by a simulated system of 44 tectonic plates. The Era button replays its geological history: one assembled supercontinent that rifts apart and drifts into 10 continents whose coastlines still fit back together. Drag to spin the globe, scroll to zoom, toggle the flat map, and click a continent to open its article.
