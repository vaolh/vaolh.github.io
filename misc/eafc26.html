Amine Samir Boukhari (born 15 March 1998) is a French professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for La Liga club Real Betis and the France national team. Known for his technical ability, vision, and precise passing, Boukhari is considered one of the most talented midfielders in European football.

Born in Lyon to Algerian parents, Boukhari began his youth career at local club FC Lyon before joining the prestigious Olympique Lyonnais academy at age 14. He started his professional career with Stade Lavallois in Ligue 2 before moving to Lyon in 2018.
