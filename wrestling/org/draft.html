The Draft

The Draft is a rotating system.

The Draft is an annual event in which each of the three sanctioning bodies, the WWF, the WWO, and the IWB, selects ten challengers per weight division from a shared pool of eligible wrestlers, locking them to that body for the year. Reigning champions are not drafted; each defends against the ten challengers drafted to their division (the field that feeds the year's contender battle royals). Priority is given to spreading opportunity across the rankings and to wrestlers who have been inactive, rather than concentrating on the pound-for-pound elite. Wrestlers left undrafted remain free agents, eligible to be signed for special-attraction bouts or cycled back into contention the following year, keeping every division fresh from season to season. Wrestlers who lose their careers in a Lucha de Apuestas are retired and removed from the pool.
